# 11-03 Resume Writing for Desktop Support

> [!abstract] Overview
> A comprehensive guide to building resumes for Desktop Support Engineers. This note covers resume structures, ATS parsing guidelines, technical keyword selection, and professional resume templates.

---

## 1. What Is It? (Concept Explanation)
A resume is a marketing document that summarizes your technical skills, work experience, and certifications. In corporate recruitment, resumes are parsed by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before being reviewed by hiring managers. Resumes must contain relevant industry keywords and measurable metrics to pass screening phases.
*Seedha simple shabdon mein: Resume aapka first impression hai. Corporate companies mein softwares (ATS) resume ko scan karte hain keywords (jaise Active Directory, Office 365, Ticketing, Troubleshooting) dhoondhne ke liye. Agar aapka resume simple format mein ho aur usme metrics (jaise 95% SLA compliance ya 100+ laptops imaged) likhe hon, toh selection ke chances 80% badh jate hain.*

---

## 2. ATS Optimization Guidelines
To ensure your resume passes automated screening software:
- **Use Simple Formatting:** Avoid complex tables, text boxes, icons, and multi-column templates. ATS readers parse files linearly and can lose text inside graphical structures.
- **Use Standard Section Headers:** Use clear titles like **Professional Experience**, **Technical Skills**, **Certifications**, and **Education**.
- **Incorporate Industry Keywords:** Match the job description. Common keywords for Desktop Support include:
  - Active Directory, Entra ID (Azure AD), Group Policy (GPO).
  - Microsoft 365, Exchange Online, Microsoft Teams, SharePoint.
  - Windows 10/11, macOS, Linux, SCCM, Intune, Windows Autopilot.
  - ITSM, ServiceNow, Jira, SLA Compliance, Incident Management.
  - Hardware Diagnostics, RAM, SSD, BIOS, BitLocker, DHCP, DNS, TCP/IP.

## 4. Study & Interview Q&A
**Q1: How do you format resume bullet points to show your business value?**
A: I use the **XYZ formula** (Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]). Instead of writing "Responsible for staging computers," I write: "Imaged and configured 150+ laptops [X], reducing setup times by 35% [Y], using Windows Autopilot and Microsoft Intune deployments [Z]." This highlights metrics and tools.

**Q2: Why is it important to customize your resume for every job application?**
A: Different organizations use different technical stacks and tools. Customizing my resume allows me to align my experience with their job description. If a company requests "Microsoft Intune" and "ServiceNow," I ensure those terms are highlighted in my skills and work experience sections to pass the ATS screening.
